Details

The Dark Age of Nevia is a series of short stories that I write for a hobby. While I would love for this fantasy world to be a graphic novel, video game, or a TTRPG, a lack of artist talent, time, money, or other such necessary resources make that next to impossible. Here is a prologue I created while experimenting in Unity, which was originally created with C# code and the Unity engine. I recreated it here in Javascript. There are some minor differences between the two; fonts, corrected content, button placement. However, the main story and functionality remains intact.
